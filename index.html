Background

My name is Lyubomir, I grew up in a small town in Bulgaria, left home at 14 for a math high school, and moved to Germany at 19. After studying CS at TU Munich and spending six years there, I joined Yelp in Hamburg, eventually growing past Senior into a Staff Software Engineer role.
