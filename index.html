About me

I grew up in Bulgaria, left home at 14 for a math high school, and moved to Germany at 19. I studied Computer Science at TU Munich and graduated with M.Sc (Distinction). After that I joined Yelp in Hamburg and worked on Ads for six years, going from Software Engineer to Staff Software Engineer. I took a sabbatical in mid‑2025, moved back to Bulgaria, and started working independently in 2026.
