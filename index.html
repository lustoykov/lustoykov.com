The Blueprint

Background: I left my $200K/year job to go all in.
The Mission: Four engines feeding into each other. I'm creating the best tech content I can today, building the software of tomorrow, and streaming the whole journey in real-time.

1. Tech YouTube

Growth Engine

Focus: Community & Education. My main hub for high-value tutorials and tech deep-dives.

The Plan: Provide massive value for free. Eventually, I’ll sell premium courses, but education comes first.

The Rule: No Selling Out. I stay independent so I can be brutally honest about the tech I review. I don’t take sponsorships that force me to be biased.

Visit Channel ↗

2. Portfolio of Apps

Product Engine

Focus: Shipping multiple products & Rapid Iteration.

The Strategy: I am building a portfolio of apps. This allows me to test different ideas, diversify my risk, and find what truly resonates with users.

The Goal: To ship consistently and build a suite of useful tools, using each launch to learn and refine my development skills.

3. Streaming

Accountability Engine

Focus: Deep Work & Life Logging. A community for high-performer builders, workers and students.

The Why: Accountability & Memories. It forces consistency, but it’s also a digital time capsule. I want to look back years from now and remember that this journey was hella fun.

The Vision: Focused work will always be the "main menu." However, I am eventually expanding into a "Life Stream" once I reach Level 3 - broadcasting morning routines, workouts, and seaside walks. The goal is a 24/7 window into the full lifestyle, not just the grind.

Watch Streams ↗

4. Daily Shorts

BTS Engine

Focus: Raw, Uncut, Behind-the-Scenes & Interests.

The Plan: Daily unpolished shorts on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and X.

The Content: Updates on the "portfolio of apps" journey, plus random thoughts on whatever interests me at the moment.

  • Authenticity: Experimenting with raw human content vs. edited/AI content.
  • Efficiency: Documenting the journey and learning content creation in the time I have.
  • Distribution: Building a top-of-funnel channel while learning to express thoughts clearly and tell stories.
Instagram ↗ TikTok ↗ X (Twitter) ↗
